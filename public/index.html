📖 How to Play

🎲 Movement

Roll the dice to move your token around the board. Each player has their own colored track.

⚔️ Duels

Land on an opponent's token to trigger a riddle duel. Answer faster to win and send them back to base!

😵 Stunned

If you lose a duel, you're stunned and sent back to your base. Roll again to escape.

🏁 Victory

First player to reach the center HOME and complete the path wins the game!

💎 Rewards

Win matches to earn coins and XP. Use coins to buy avatars and abilities in the shop!
