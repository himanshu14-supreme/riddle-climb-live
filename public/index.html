    📜 How to Play

    • Objective: Reach cell #1 to win the race!
    • The Roll: The host rolls for a riddle. Everyone sees the same question.
    • Speed Matters: The faster you answer correctly, the more steps you climb:
      • 🥇 1st Correct: +3 Steps
      • 🥈 2nd Correct: +2 Steps
      • 🥉 3rd Correct: +1 Step
    • Wrong Answers: No movement for incorrect guesses or timeouts.
