📖 How to Play

🎲 Movement

Roll a 6 to bring a token out of the base. Roll again to move!

⚔️ Duels

Land on an opponent's token to trigger a riddle duel. Answer faster to win and send them back to base!

😵 Stunned

If you lose a duel, your token returns to base and you miss your next turn (unless you have a shield).

🏁 Victory

First player to get all 4 tokens to the center HOME wins!
